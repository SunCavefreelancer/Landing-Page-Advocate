How to conclude an agreement:

The agreement is a mandatory condition for the provision of legal assistance. Such a document is usually concluded in writing on paper. However, if the client is unable to come in person to the office or hand over a signed copy of the agreement to the lawyer, such an agreement can be concluded in electronic form using an electronic digital signature of the person who entered into such an agreement, with its subsequent sending to the lawyer by means of electronic communication or communication.
